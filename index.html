Photo CNN · Rescue operations at the Córrego do Feijão tailings dam, Vale, Brumadinho, Minas Gerais, Brazil. January 25, 2019.

In the 47 days before Vale's Córrego do Feijão tailings dam collapsed — killing 270 people and releasing 12 million cubic meters of iron ore waste — InSAR satellite data recorded surface deformation accelerating at 3.4mm per day. The data existed. The monitoring system worked. The legal architecture did not.
