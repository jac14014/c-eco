c-ECO

c-ECO is a research framework and institutional platform focused on contractual governance, systemic risk, and ecological co-responsibility in a planetary economy.

The framework reframes contracts as micro-governance architectures, capable of allocating authority, responsibility, and adaptive decision points ex ante — before systemic risk and ecological harm become irreversible.
