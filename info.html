c-ECO

Contractual Equity & Ecological Co-Responsibility

c-ECO is an original research framework and institutional platform focused on contractual governance, systemic risk, and ecological co-responsibility in a planetary economy.

The framework conceptualizes contracts as micro-governance architectures, capable of allocating authority, responsibility, and adaptive decision points ex ante — before systemic risk and ecological harm become irreversible.

c-ECO engages with foundations, research institutions, policy labs, and development actors through research partnerships, applied Concept Notes, pilot governance frameworks, and institutional fellowships focused on pre-threshold decision-making and systemic risk governance.

For institutional partnerships and research inquiries, a detailed Concept Note is available upon request.
