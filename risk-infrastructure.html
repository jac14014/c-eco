Risk Matrix & Mitigation
Key-Person Dependency
High

Project depends on single founder for vision, partnerships, and execution.

Mitigation: Hire Science-Policy Lead and Integration Officer within 60 days. Document all TFP protocols. Establish GIH succession plan.

Partner Formalization Delay
Medium

INPA, MPEG, or ACTO may delay MOU execution due to institutional bureaucracy.

Mitigation: Start with Letter of Intent (1 page). Parallel track with 2 institutions. Use Hasse Foundation board connections.

Currency & Inflation Volatility
Medium

BRL volatility may erode purchasing power for local operations and salaries.

Mitigation: Hold 40% of local costs in USD reserve. Index salaries to IPCA. Use multi-currency accounting.

Technology & Data Access
Medium

Satellite data gaps, sensor failure in remote areas, or INPE access restrictions.

Mitigation: Multi-source redundancy (Planet + Sentinel + Landsat). Spare sensor inventory. Local technician training.

Political & Regulatory Shift
Medium

Changes in Brazilian environmental policy may affect Forest Code enforcement or data access.

Mitigation: Diversify regulatory hooks (subnational, private sector, international treaties). Maintain non-partisan positioning.

Funding Continuity
High

18-month runway with no guaranteed Beta phase funding creates cliff-edge risk.

Mitigation: Begin Beta fundraising at Month 6. Develop revenue model (TFP licensing, enterprise contracts). Build endowment case.
