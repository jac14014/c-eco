Analytical Framework
This document constitutes a formal request for engagement under the c-ECO systemic governance framework. Applicants may select from three minimum-viable deployment options (A/B/C), each calibrated for distinct organizational readiness levels and risk governance maturity.

CLAUSE I — Engagement Options
OPTION A
c-ECO Rapid Risk Scan
1.1 Duration & Deliverable
Approximately 2 weeks. Systemic risk diagnostic plus TFP band mapping (Green/Amber/Red) for one operation, contract, or supply chain segment.
1.2 Scope Limitations
Single point of analysis. Does not include multi-stakeholder negotiation protocols or board-level governance restructuring.
OPTION B
Contract Addendum Package
2.1 Duration & Deliverable
Approximately 3–4 weeks. Clause package incorporating c-ECO systemic governance principles, negotiation playbook, and band-based obligations matrix (TFP).
2.2 Legal Integration
Designed for incorporation by reference into existing contractual frameworks. Includes Safe Mode triggers, Functional Novation protocols, and IEX activation clauses.
OPTION C
Board Briefing + Governance Setup
3.1 Duration & Deliverable
Approximately 1 month. Board-level governance framework, early signal detection protocols, trajectory duties definition, and lightweight implementation roadmap.
3.2 Fiduciary Alignment
Addresses duty-of-care alignment with systemic risk governance. Includes board-ready risk narrative (non-technical) and escalation protocol design.
