c-ECO Systemic Governance Initiative
Fellowship → Enterprise Pathway

From applied cases to enterprise deployment.

The c-ECO Fellowship develops real-world systemic governance cases. c-ECO Enterprise turns those validated cases into actionable protocols for organizations.

One architecture. Two entry points.

The Fellowship is not separate from Enterprise. It is the applied research pipeline through which c-ECO develops, tests, and refines sector-specific cases. Enterprise is the implementation pathway for organizations ready to apply the framework to live contracts, risk systems, governance structures, or operational decisions.

The c-ECO Pathway

How knowledge becomes deployment

01 — Fellowship

Case Development

Fellows work on selected real-world sectors, systems, or institutional problems.

  • Applied case research
  • Sector mapping
  • Risk and dependency identification
  • Pre-threshold legal framing
→
02 — Validation Layer

Framework Translation

Fellowship outputs are translated into c-ECO instruments, including TFP logic, contractual triggers, Safe Mode pathways, and governance protocols.

  • TFP / TDR mapping
  • Recognition–Action Gap analysis
  • Contractual trigger architecture
  • Board-ready narratives
→
03 — Enterprise

Deployment

Organizations engage c-ECO Enterprise to apply the framework to a concrete operation, contract, portfolio, project, or institutional risk system.

  • Rapid Risk Scan
  • Contract Addendum
  • Board Briefing
  • Sector-specific pilot design

Fellowship creates the applied intelligence. Enterprise activates it.
