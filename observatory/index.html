Observatory is the publication space of c-ECO. It presents factual analyses and institutional reflections on legality, governability, and systemic risk, grounded in observable events rather than normative prescriptions.


2025 Retrospective

This retrospective examines five factual observations from 2025 that reveal a recurring structural pattern: the persistence of legal authorization even after the erosion of material governability.

Drawing on concrete developments across climate, infrastructure, technology, and regulation, the publication shows how legality may remain formally intact while the decision-space required to prevent irreversible harm has already been consumed.

The analysis supports the need for legal and contractual instruments capable of intervening before governability collapses, informing public policy, contractual architecture, and long-horizon institutional decision-making.
