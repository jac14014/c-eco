G.0.1 Staffing Model

Each Living Lab operates on a hybrid Core + Extended model. Core Team members are 100% funded and dedicated full-time, ensuring institutional continuity. Extended Network members are affiliated specialists contributing on-demand.

# Role Function Typical Profile
1 Science-Policy Lead Scientific direction and policy interface PhD in Earth System Science / domain specialist with governance experience
2 c-ECO Integration Officer Translates lab outputs into c-ECO protocols Complex systems specialist, systemic risk modeling
3–6 Domain Experts (3–4) Technical specialization in lab areas PhDs or equivalent in specific disciplines
7–8 Data/AI Engineers (2) Data infrastructure, modeling, ML Data engineers with geospatial / climate AI experience
9–10 Legal/Regulatory Specialists (1–2) Compliance, regulatory hooks, contracts International, environmental, or financial law
11–12 Field Operations Coordinators (2) Field logistics, local partnerships, data collection Project managers with adverse conditions / local context experience
13 Network Liaison Interface with other labs, GIH, institutional ecosystem Diplomacy / institutional relations

Total per lab: 7–10 Core + 25–50 Extended | GIH: 24 Core + 60 Extended
