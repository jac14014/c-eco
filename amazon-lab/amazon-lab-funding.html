Core Narrative Shift: All materials are repositioned from "climate innovation project" to "financial risk infrastructure." The Amazon Lab is no longer "research" — it is the missing layer between ecological detection and financial action. This changes the conversation from "why fund this?" to "why haven't we funded this already?"
